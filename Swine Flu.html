Swine Flu

Swine Flu

Swine flu is an infection caused by any one of several types of swine influenza viruses. This was one of the major events of our ngo - hind towards change. This programme was basically held for spreading awareness about swine flu among the school kids of a small village, potheri.

Being a village, potheri is full of dirt and stray animals. Swine influenza virus is common throughout pig populations worldwide .so, we decided to go and visit the school and spread awareness regarding swine flu .

The target audience for our event was school kids. We went to them and told them about the symptoms, treatment and prevention from this hazardous disease. We told children about the duration of the disease. Swine flu typically begins to resolve after three to seven days, but the malaise and cough can persist two weeks or more in some patients. By watching the interest and curiosity in the kids, we told them the general symptoms of swine flu, so that not only will they be able to protect themselves, but also save others.

So, we tried our best to spread awareness about such a horrible disease among kids. Then ,we provide them with some juices and biscuits . To make them comfortable, we also talked about their hobbies and dislikes.

The main objective of our event was to create some awareness and share information about swine flu, which is not much known in a country like India. Swine flu is considered as one of the dangerous disease, which can even cause death.
